Frequently Asked Questions

1. How does AI color analysis work?
Our AI analyzes your uploaded photo by detecting your undertone, contrast level, and facial features, then matches you to a seasonal color palette (like “Warm Autumn” or “Cool Summer”) based on real color theory.
2. Do I need to wear makeup in my photo?
Actually, no! For best results, we recommend a bare-faced photo in natural lighting, with your hair pulled away from your face.
3. Can I use a photo I already have?
Yes—just make sure it’s clear, taken in soft daylight, and not heavily filtered or edited.
4. What happens after I get my result?
You’ll receive your color season, a custom palette, helpful styling tips, and curated product suggestions tailored to your tones.
5. How accurate is the AI?
Our AI gives a strong, research-based result, but since color is personal, we also offer expert consultations if you'd like deeper guidance.
6. Can I talk to a real color consultant?
Yes! You can book a virtual consultation with a certified expert who can walk you through your result and give personalized advice.
7. Will I get makeup and clothing recommendations?
Definitely—we suggest beauty and fashion items that match your palette, and we’re working on partnerships with real brands too.
8. Will this tell me what colors to avoid?
Yes, along with your best shades, we’ll highlight tones that might not complement your coloring, so you can shop smarter.
9. Is this only for women?
Not at all! Color analysis works for all genders, styles, and anyone curious about looking and feeling better.
10. Is my photo safe?
Absolutely. We don’t store or share your image—it's used only once for analysis, then immediately deleted.
11. Can I retake the test later?
Of course! You can try again any time—especially if your look changes, like new hair color or a tan.
12. What if I don’t like my result?
That’s okay! Think of this as a tool, not a rule—trust your own instincts, and reach out to an expert if you’d like support.
13. Can I use this on my phone?
Yes—our platform is mobile-friendly, so you can upload and explore your results on any device.
14. Can I share my results on social media?
Absolutely! You’ll have the option to share your palette or product recommendations directly with friends.
15. What makes ColorSnap different from quizzes or filters?
We combine real AI tech with certified color consultant insight—not just a trend-based quiz, but something you can actually apply in daily life.
16. Do I need to pay to use it?
The basic analysis is free. If you want to go deeper with expert support or premium features, we offer optional upgrades.
17. Can my color season change over time?
Yes—factors like aging, tanning, or hair color changes can shift your palette slightly, so it's worth checking in again once in a while.
18. Will you offer more features in the future?
Yes! We’re working on smarter shopping tools, more expert content, and language options to serve a global community.
19. Do you keep or store my photos?
No, we don’t store any uploaded images. Your photo is used one time for the AI to generate your result and is automatically deleted afterward.
20. Will my personal data be shared with anyone?
Never. We don’t sell, share, or give your personal data to third parties. Your privacy matters to us as much as your perfect color palette does.
21. Is the platform secure to use?
Yes. All photo uploads and user data are processed through encrypted, secure channels to ensure your information stays private.
22. Do I need to make an account to use ColorSnap?
Nope! You can try the basic analysis without signing up. If you choose to save your results or book a consultation, we may ask for minimal info to make the experience smoother—but never more than necessary.
